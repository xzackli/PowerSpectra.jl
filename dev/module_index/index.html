AngularPowerSpectra.Œûsum ‚Äî Method
Œûsum(alm‚ÇÅ, alm‚ÇÇ, w3j‚ÇÅ, w3j‚ÇÇ)

Sum over $\ell$ and $m$ of two $a_{\ell m}$ and nontrivial Wigner-3j vectors. This is a step in computing the $\mathbf{\Xi}$ matrix. The $\rho$ factors are not in this summation, as they can be pulled out.

\[\begin{aligned} (\Xi \mathrm{sum}) &= \sum_{\ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}} \, \left(_{s_1+\nu_1}\tilde{\omega}^{(j_1)}_{\ell^\prime m^\prime}\right) \left(_{s_2+\nu_2}\tilde{\omega}^{(j_2)}_{\ell^\prime m^\prime}\right)^* \\ &\qquad\qquad \times \begin{pmatrix} \ell & \ell^{\prime} & \ell^{\prime\prime} \\ -s_1 & s_1+\nu_1 & -\nu_1 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \ell & \ell^{\prime} & \ell^{\prime\prime} \\ -s_2 & s_2+\nu_2 & -\nu_2 \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned}\]
